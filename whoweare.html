Meet Our Members

Why Fiji Blue? It's the color of the emotions we try to convery through our music -- light blue can bring serenity while dark blue brings a sense of melancholy.

Trevor Dering

Singer-Songwriter

As a songwriter, Trevor hopes to create something from nothing. What sets him apart from other artists is his ability to contribute towards many aspects in the creation process of a song.
His newest track, "Peppermint," is dedicated to his love for his wife, who is the key creative inspiration for the album.

Valentin Fritz

Producer

Valentin is a freelance producer, DJ, and songwriter. Music has been a part of his life for as long as he could remember. From Big Bands to Orchestras, he's been involved in arranging and writing for different ensembles.
Valentin met Trevor at a party at Berklee, and the two showed great chemistry with their passion for music. Besides being a duo with Trevor in Fiji Blue, Valentin has worked with Bon Jovi, Dylan, and Emilia Ali.
